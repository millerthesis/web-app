Most Gentrified Areas

These are areas of the United States that rank highly on the Gentrification Index. This index was created based on former analyses on gentrification by Daniel Hartley at the Cleveland Federal Reserve and Governing magazine. The index uses data from the Census to calculate levels of gentrification, specifically: populations of white and nonwhite people; median age; median home values; percent of owner-occupied housing units; percent of people with at least a bachelor's degree; and income per capita. You can read about the background and analysis considerations on the Gentrification Index page.
