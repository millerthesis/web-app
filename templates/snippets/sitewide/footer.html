About this project

This app was created as a master's thesis project for the Stanford Journalism Program. The app uses historical Census data to better visualize gentrification in the United States.

The homepage provides an overview of places that have dynamic rates of change for data points related to gentrification. As a practical tool, you can view your own neighborhood, city or state to see how demographics and populations have changed over time. Or, view data by category: demographics, housing, income and residency.
