About this project

This app was created for a master's thesis project for the Stanford Journalism Program. Interested in identifying areas of gentrification, I sought to compare historical Census data surveys (ACS and Decennial surveys) to better visualize areas of intense gentrification.

As a practical tool, you can view your own neighborhood, city or state to see how demographics and populations have changed over time.
