Methodology

Data

This app uses data collected by the United States Census Bureau. Data comes from the (1990/2000/2010) Decennial and the 5-year American Community Surveys for 2005-2009 and 2009-2016 .

Analysis

Gentrification is generally defined as the process of wealthier residents moving into urban areas and displacing the existing communities, and often falls on racial and economic lines.

Data points were chosen to measure gentrification based on similar analyses by Governing and CityLab. Changes in median income levels, property values, race and education are used as proxies for measuring the impact of gentrification.

Limitations

TK caveats about comparing across Census surveys and years - some questions have changed, not all questions have identical questions, etc.
