Visualizing Gentrification

Gentrification is generally described as the process of wealthier individuals moving into an existing community, a process that often increases property values, rent prices, and changes the culture and demography of the area.

While gentrified areas may become safer, see an increase in economic opportunity and have better schools, gentrification also leads to displacement of the poorer communities that existed previously.

This app provides an overview of gentrifying areas in the United States using Census data. You can also enter an address, city or state to receive data about that place.
